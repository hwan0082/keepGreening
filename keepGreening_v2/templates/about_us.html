About us

We are team Huwalir, a group consisted of four Monash Master students. We care about the environment and want to make some changes. KeepGreening is our Industry Experience project, it aims to provide those environment-caring Victorians to build their sustainable garden.

Our mission

Our mission is to provide the most useful and effective solution of sustainable gardening to those responsible residents across Victoria in order to build more climate-friendly gardens.
